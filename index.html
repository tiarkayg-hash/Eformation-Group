Limited Liability Option

Clients may declare a value for parcels and pay a small fee for limited liability.

Maximum liability is R50,000 per waybill.

Many items are excluded, including cash, jewellery, electronics, alcohol, perishables, vehicles, livestock, hazardous goods, and more.

If parcels are not properly packaged, claims may be rejected.

Insurance
